Essential Appointment Guidelines

First-Time Guests
Arriving for your initial visit? Please plan to arrive at least 5 minutes early to complete a brief intake form, ensuring your experience starts seamlessly.

Appointment Confirmation
All appointments require confirmation to guarantee your reserved time. You’ll receive a courtesy reminder via text or email three days in advance—simply reply to confirm or let us know if you need to reschedule.

Running Late
To respect everyone’s schedule, we may not be able to accommodate arrivals more than 10 minutes late. If you anticipate being delayed, please call us as soon as possible; when feasible, we’ll offer the next available slot. When driving, allow extra time for traffic and parking.
